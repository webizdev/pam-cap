About Us

Bridging International Capital and Indonesian Enterprise

PT Pamilih Capital Advisory (PCA) is an independent financial advisory firm based in Jakarta, specializing in private capital transactions in Indonesia.

We advise international investors, financial institutions, and growth companies on private equity investments, structured financing, and strategic capital solutions.

Our Work

Driving Economic Transformation

Our work focuses on sectors central to Indonesia’s long-term economic transformation, including energy transition, digital infrastructure, financial services, and sustainable infrastructure.

In a market where high-quality opportunities often require careful structuring, governance alignment, and trusted local insight, PCA provides the analytical discipline and senior advisory required to bridge international capital and Indonesian enterprise.

Advisory Services

Strategic Focus Areas

Private Capital Advisory

PCA advises on strategic private capital transactions involving Indonesian companies and international investors.

Our services include:

  • Buy-side and sell-side advisory
  • Capital raising for growth companies
  • Strategic partnerships and joint ventures
  • Minority and control investments
  • Exit strategy planning and transaction structuring

We focus on transactions where long-term capital, governance alignment, and disciplined execution are essential to success.

Debt & Structured Finance

We structure financing solutions for corporate expansion, infrastructure development, and capital restructuring.

Our advisory services include:

  • Project and infrastructure finance
  • Senior and mezzanine financing
  • Blended finance structures
  • Debt restructuring and refinancing
  • Capital structure optimization

PCA works with international lenders, private credit funds, and development finance institutions to design financing structures aligned with project risk and investor requirements.

Sustainable & Energy Transition Finance

Indonesia’s transition toward a lower-carbon economy presents significant investment opportunities.

PCA advises on transactions involving:

  • Renewable energy platforms
  • Energy transition infrastructure
  • Green bond structuring
  • Climate and impact investment initiatives

Our advisory approach integrates sustainability considerations with rigorous financial analysis.

Digital & Financial Infrastructure

Indonesia’s digital economy is among the fastest growing in Southeast Asia.

We advise investors and companies on opportunities involving:

  • Fintech and digital banking platforms
  • P2P and embedded finance ecosystems
  • Data centres and digital infrastructure
  • Technology-enabled financial services

Our focus is on scalable platforms capable of attracting institutional capital.
